🛠️ The best technical leaders choose tools that amplify discipline, reduce cognitive load, and keep the team moving.

- Prefer toolchains that support repeatable workflows over one-off hacks.
- Use automation to remove friction, not to replace thoughtful review.
- Select platforms that make operational visibility and cost control explicit.

Good tooling is not glamorous. It is the quiet foundation that lets teams ship quality work without constant firefighting.
